DPAD Messaging is a privacy-focused SMS and MMS messaging app designed for Android TV devices. Navigate your conversations entirely with a D-pad remote — no touchscreen needed.

Features:
• Send and receive SMS and MMS messages
• Full D-pad navigation optimized for TV remotes
• Block unwanted messages by phone number or keyword
• Backup and restore all messages, settings, and blocked lists to a JSON file
• Multiple accent themes: Blue, Green, Orange, Rose
• Schedule messages to be sent later
• Recycle bin for accidentally deleted messages
• Dark theme support
• No ads, no tracking, no unnecessary permissions
• Open source under the MIT license
